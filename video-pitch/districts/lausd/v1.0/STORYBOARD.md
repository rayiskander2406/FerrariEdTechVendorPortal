## VISUAL STYLE GUIDE

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| LAUSD Gold | #FFB81C | Accents, highlights, CTA |
| LAUSD Navy | #003087 | Headers, important text |
| White | #FFFFFF | Background, clean space |
| Success Green | #22C55E | Checkmarks, positive indicators |
| Risk Red | #EF4444 | Problems, warnings |
| Neutral Gray | #64748B | Secondary text |

### Typography
- **Headers**: Bold sans-serif (Inter Bold, Poppins Bold)
- **Body**: Regular sans-serif (Inter Regular)
- **Numbers/Stats**: Bold, slightly larger for impact

### Animations
- **Transitions**: Smooth fades, subtle zoom (0.3s duration)
- **Data visualization**: Numbers count up
- **Icons**: Subtle bounce on appear
- **Emphasis**: Gentle pulse on key words

### Sound Design
- **Background**: Subtle corporate ambient (low, unobtrusive)
- **Accents**: Soft "pop" on checkmarks, "whoosh" on transitions
- **Voice**: Professional male or female, executive tone
